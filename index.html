                            I Rise                        

    The sun was not bright enough

    To light up the darkness in which I was Engulfed

    Eyes swollen Skin sunken I became withered

    As failures increased to grow upon me

    Pain in my heart was plastered by a smile on my face

    Going in a rat race, I forgot my own pace

    Fighting a lost battle I didn't realise

    I Was the only army and I was my own enemy

    I couldn't win the game Neither could I, the name

    All I won was shame, Which was never my aim

    As everyone looked at me

    In disdain I drowned more in pain

    I forgot doing everything I love

    For that didn't make any sense now

    The skies cry for me in the form of rain

    Or so felt As I now stopped

    Admiring the little things of beauty

    The night became my only friend

    In whom I conceded to any end

    Wished I Could be just one star

    In the large universe

    But what failed to understand was

    I was the sun, who lights the darkness up

    And there could be no brighter sun

    Than me for myself.

    And that yearns to listen that

    Cause you're also a hu" man".
